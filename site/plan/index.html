Common Questions

Is Carry-On really free?

Yes. Create one trip suitcase with up to 5 stops and use all core planning tools for free. No credit card needed.

What does $9.99 one-time mean?

You pay once for First Class access. No monthly fees, no subscription, no renewal.

Can I plan more than one trip?

Yes. First Class includes unlimited trip suitcases.

Can I print my itinerary?

Yes. First Class includes PDF export of your full itinerary.

Is Northlander.app separate from NorthlanderGuide.com?

Yes. NorthlanderGuide.com helps you discover the route. Northlander.app helps you save and organise your trip.

Do I need a train ticket to use it?

No. You can plan your trip before booking your ticket.
