Chapter Five

Connected to the Guide

Northlander.app works hand-in-hand with NorthlanderGuide.com. Every restaurant, event and place on the guide is one tap away from your trip.

Add from the Guide in one tap

Every restaurant, event, accommodation and attraction on NorthlanderGuide.com has a plus button. Tap it to drop the place straight into your trip suitcase, no copy-pasting.

Train times built in

Boarding and arrival times for every Northlander stop are populated automatically. You just add your own activities around the train schedule.

Your own time slots

Drop activities into the windows between trains. The app keeps you on schedule and flags you when something runs past the next departure.

Works offline

Once your trip is loaded, your itinerary lives in your pocket. Open it anywhere, anytime, even when the train passes through a no-signal stretch of Northern Ontario.

Chapter Nine

Common Questions

Is Carry-On really free?

Yes. Create one trip suitcase with up to 5 stops and use all core planning tools for free. No credit card needed.

What does $9.99 one-time mean?

You pay once for First Class access. No monthly fees, no subscription, no renewal.

Can I plan more than one trip?

Yes. First Class includes unlimited trip suitcases.

Can I print my itinerary?

Yes. First Class includes PDF export of your full itinerary.

Is Northlander.app separate from NorthlanderGuide.com?

Yes. NorthlanderGuide.com helps you discover the route. Northlander.app helps you save and organise your trip.

Do I need a train ticket to use it?

No. You can plan your trip before booking your ticket.

Will it work if I lose signal on the train?

Yes. Once your trip is loaded, the app caches everything locally so your itinerary, packing list, notes and booking checklist stay available even when you're between cell towers. Changes you make offline sync the next time you have a connection.
